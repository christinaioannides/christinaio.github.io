Short Documentary Film

"On the Intersection of Queerness and Race" is a short documentary by Christina Ioannides and Sophia Henderson. It highlights LGBTQ+ students of color at Fordham Lincoln Center, as they discuss their experiences, struggles with identity, and the importance of a diverse community. The documentary was made in collaboration with Prism, Fordham LC's LGBTQ+ organization. It includes interviews with club leaders and members as well as footage from their club meetings and events.
